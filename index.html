Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is a Ready Lead?

A "Meeting-Ready-Lead" is a lead that expresses interest in your product or service and has scheduled a specific date or time to discuss a demo. On average, our clients convert approximately 85-95% of these leads into demos.

2. How do we handle the leads?

After generating a meeting-ready lead, we will book the meeting directly through your calendar and include the entire email conversation. Just take the call.

3. Does your team take care of management?

Yes, our team will take care of everything from inbox setup, lead list building, writing copy, managing the campaign, and handling responses.

4. What if the lead isn't qualified?

Meeting-Ready-Leads are not your typical PPC leads. These leads are highly qualified because we target sophisticated buyers who show direct interest in your product/service and are willing to schedule a call. We reach out to a lead list approved by you with a hyper-personalized message and manage responses to convert them into meeting-ready leads.
